What is StatusLex?

StatusLex was created by the Lexington Department of Planning, Preservation, and Development in order to provide a user-friendly way for citizens to view city data.

Where is this information coming from?

This data is extracted directly from City of Lexington Databases and hosted on an open data portal at CivicData.

How often is this information updated?

Most datasets are updated every 15 minutes.

How much did this cost?

Nothing!(except time) We made it ourself using open source tools. The source code of this application is freely available on GitHub
